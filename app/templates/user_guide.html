HR Administrator OperationsHR 管理员操作
Employee and attendance-device setup performed by HR administrators.HR 管理员负责员工及打卡设备配置。
Add a New Employee新增员工
Before you start: department is configured, at least one online device exists, and the current Mingdao user has employee-admin permission.前置条件：已创建部门；至少一台在线设备；当前用户具有员工管理权限。
1
Create employee profile创建员工档案
Create the employee in Mingdao and save required fields.在明道中新增员工信息并保存。
2
Workflow sends data工作流发送数据
Mingdao workflow calls the ADMS Inbound API.明道工作流调用 ADMS 对外 API。
3
ADMS creates sync jobsADMS 创建同步任务
ADMS queues jobs for every attendance device.ADMS 给所有考勤设备创建下发任务。
4
Device receives user设备接收员工
The device writes the employee on its next request.设备下次请求时写入员工信息。
Synced同步完成
Employee can be used on the target device.员工已成功同步到设备。
Verification: open ADMS Users, Device Management, or the device user list to confirm the employee exists.验证方式：在 ADMS 用户列表、设备管理或打卡机用户列表中确认员工已存在。
Enroll Fingerprint录入指纹
Before you start: employee exists on the device, and the operator has enrollment permission.前置条件：员工已存在于设备；操作者具备指纹录入权限。
1
Enroll on device在设备录入
Enroll or update the employee fingerprint on the attendance device.在打卡设备上为员工录入或更新指纹。
2
Upload fingerprint data上传指纹数据
The device uploads FP data to ADMS.设备将指纹数据上传至 ADMS。
3
ADMS saves latest templateADMS 保存最新模板
ADMS verifies and stores the newest fingerprint template.ADMS 校验并保存最新指纹模板。
4
Distribute to devices分发到其他设备
ADMS sends the template to other target devices.ADMS 将模板分发到其他目标设备。
Synced已同步
Fingerprint is available on all target devices.指纹模板已同步到所有目标设备。
If the device is offline, the sync job waits and retries when the device reconnects.设备离线时，数据保存在待同步队列，并在设备恢复后自动重试。
Set Device Role设置设备角色
1
Select device选择打卡设备
Choose the device that needs a role change.选择需要设置角色的打卡设备。
2
Find employee查找员工
Search by employee ID, PIN, name, or department.通过工号、PIN、姓名或部门查找员工。
3
Choose role选择设备角色
Select Normal, Admin, or Enroll Admin.选择普通用户、管理员或登记管理员。
4
Save and queue保存并排队
ADMS queues the role command for that device.保存设置并加入同步队列发送到设备。
Role active角色生效
The device applies the role on the next request.设备已更新角色设置并生效。
Normal users punch only. Device admins manage the device. Enroll admins can perform enrollment according to device-side role permissions.普通用户仅打卡；管理员可管理设备；登记管理员按设备端角色权限执行录入。
Add a New Device新增打卡机
1
Configure ADMS server配置服务器地址

2
Device connects ADMS设备连接 ADMS

3
Auto discovery自动发现并登记

Current devices must use UTC+4 time configuration.注意：当前设备时区按 UTC+4 配置。
System Operations系统工作
ADMS Service Administrator NotesADMS 服务管理员说明
These tasks are maintained by the ADMS administrator and do not require routine HR action.以下工作由 ADMS 服务管理员维护，HR 日常无需操作。
Backup and Restore备份与恢复

Automatic daily backups are kept on the server. Download backup packages before risky changes and use the restore guide after server failure.系统每日自动备份。服务器管理员应在高风险变更前手动备份，定期下载备份包至服务器外保存；需要恢复时按恢复说明执行并确认数据完整性。

System Alerts系统告警

The system monitors offline devices, sync failures, and backup freshness. Alerts appear in Console and can also be sent by webhook.系统持续监控设备离线、同步失败和备份过期。告警始终显示在控制台，服务器管理员可配置阈值及 Webhook，并负责跟进异常。

Daily Reconciliation每日同步

Scheduled reconciliation re-queues missing users, roles, and fingerprints for attendance devices.系统按计划自动核对设备数据，为缺失的员工、角色和指纹重新创建同步任务。服务器管理员只需查看执行结果并处理持续失败的设备。

Device Role设备角色Permission Meaning权限含义
Normal User普通用户Punch attendance only.仅用于打卡。
Device Admin设备管理员Can manage the attendance device according to firmware permissions.可按设备固件权限管理打卡机。
Enroll Admin登记管理员ADMS assigns the enrollment role group. Detailed role permissions are configured directly on the attendance device.ADMS 负责分配登记管理员角色组；具体可打开哪些设备菜单，需要在打卡机本机的角色权限中配置。
